About Étoile Restaurant

Welcome to Étoile Restaurant, an elegant oasis where culinary art meets a welcoming atmosphere. Located in the heart of the city, our bistro offers a delightful escape from the daily hustle, transporting you to a serene environment with a touch of French charm.

  • Culinary Excellence at Étoile Restaurant.
  • Elegant and Welcoming Ambiance.
  • Unforgettable Gastronomic Experience.

Indulge in our selection of fine wines and handcrafted cocktails, carefully paired to complement the flavors of our cuisine. At Étoile Restaurant, every detail is designed to delight your senses and create lasting memories.

Shrimp & Rice Garden Medley

Pair this dish with a crisp Sauvignon Blanc to enhance the herbal notes and complement the shrimp's sweetness.

Succulent shrimp sautéed with aromatic herbs, served over fluffy steamed rice and accompanied by a fresh mixed greens salad. This dish offers a delightful blend of ocean flavors and crisp textures, perfect for a light yet satisfying meal.

Tropical Mint Fruit Salad

Enjoy this salad chilled and consider adding a drizzle of honey or a sprinkle of toasted coconut flakes for extra sweetness and texture.

A refreshing combination of juicy pineapple chunks, vibrant orange slices, sweet blueberries, and a hint of fresh peppermint. This colorful fruit salad bursts with tropical flavors and a cooling mint finish, ideal as a starter or a palate cleanser.

Grilled Chicken Pasta Primavera

Add a squeeze of fresh lemon juice and a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese just before serving to elevate the flavors.

Tender grilled chicken strips served atop al dente pasta tossed in a light garlic and olive oil sauce with seasonal vegetables. This classic dish harmoniously blends simplicity and taste, offering comfort in every bite.

Holiday Cheesy Pasta Bake

Serve with a side of garlic bread and a robust red wine to complement the richness of the cheese sauce.

Chunky pasta baked in a rich cheese sauce with roasted grape tomatoes, creating a golden, bubbly delight. This hearty casserole is a festive favorite, perfect for warming gatherings during the Christmas season.

Berry Mango Green Smoothie

For added creaminess and protein, include a spoonful of Greek yogurt or almond butter.

A nutritious blend of sweet blueberries, ripe bananas, juicy mangoes, and fresh spinach leaves. This vibrant smoothie is packed with antioxidants and vitamins, offering a delicious and energizing start to your day.
